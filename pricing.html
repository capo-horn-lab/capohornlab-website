FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What do I get with the Free plan?

Full access to one featured research study — charts, methodology, trade statistics, and verdict included. It's our way of demonstrating the quality and depth of our work before you commit to a subscription.

What's included in the Research Subscription?

Unlimited access to every published study in our library. Each study includes a complete research document with methodology, charts, trade statistics (Sharpe, CAGR, Max DD, Win Rate), equity curves, downloadable PDFs, raw CSV exports, and new research as it's published.

How do the Backtest subscriptions work?

They are monthly recurring subscriptions per instrument. Select your tier based on data depth: Standard (OHLC + trades), Advanced (MBP-10 order book), or Advanced Plus (MBO full order book). You can cancel at any time. Each tier includes automated monthly reports and ongoing access to the backtesting pipeline for that instrument.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my Backtest tier?

Yes. You can move between tiers at any time. Changes take effect at the start of the next billing cycle.

Can I subscribe to research without buying a backtest?

Absolutely. The Research Subscription is standalone for reading and downloading published studies. Backtest services are separate — subscribe per instrument when you need ongoing strategy analysis.

What instruments do you support?

We support major US futures including ES (S&P 500 E-mini), NQ (Nasdaq 100 E-mini), CL (Crude Oil), 6E (Euro FX), YM (Dow), and others. Contact us for instruments not listed.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, anytime. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period. No long-term contracts, no cancellation fees.

Do you offer team or enterprise plans?

We do. If you need multi-seat research access or multi-instrument backtest bundles, contact us for custom pricing.
