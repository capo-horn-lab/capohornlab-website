1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, tablet, smartphone) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently, enhance user experience, and provide information to website owners. Cookies may be "first-party" (set by the website you are visiting) or "third-party" (set by a domain other than the website you are visiting).

This policy also covers other tracking technologies, including local storage, session storage, web beacons, and pixel tags, which function similarly to cookies and may be used on our Platform.

3. Purpose of Cookies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies for the following purposes:

  • Strictly Necessary: To enable core functionality such as authentication, security, and session management. These cookies are essential for the Platform to operate and do not require your prior consent under the ePrivacy Directive.
  • Functional: To remember your preferences and settings, making your experience more convenient and personalised.
  • Analytics: To understand how our Platform is used — which features are most popular, where users encounter friction, and how we can improve the service. Analytics data is aggregated and anonymised.
  • Marketing: To show you relevant Capo Horn Lab content on third-party platforms and measure the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns. These cookies are only placed after you have given explicit consent.

5. Your Consent (ePrivacy & GDPR)

In accordance with the ePrivacy Directive (Article 5(3)) and the GDPR, we follow a layered consent model:

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: These are set automatically as they are essential for the Platform to function. No prior consent is required under applicable law.
  • Functional and Analytics Cookies: These are set after you have been informed via our cookie consent banner and have taken affirmative action (e.g., clicking "Accept All" or saving your specific preferences).
  • Marketing Cookies: These are set only upon your explicit prior consent via the cookie consent banner.

You may withdraw or modify your consent at any time by clicking the "Cookie Preferences" link in the footer of our Platform or by adjusting your browser settings as described in Section 6 below.

6. How to Manage Cookies

You have full control over cookies placed on your device. You can manage your preferences in the following ways:

6.1 Our Cookie Preference Centre

You can adjust your cookie preferences at any time by clicking the "Cookie Preferences" link in the footer of any page on our Platform. This allows you to grant or withdraw consent for each category of cookies (except strictly necessary).

6.2 Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can typically:

  • View which cookies are stored on your device and delete individual or all cookies.
  • Block third-party cookies.
  • Block all cookies (note: this will impact the functionality of our Platform).
  • Set the browser to notify you when a cookie is being set.

For instructions, refer to your browser's help section:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge
